## Boundary before model

The model choice matters, but the operating boundary decides whether the system can be approved. Define where data lives, where adaptation happens, where inference runs, and what evidence remains.

## Review questions

- What cannot leave the environment?
- Who can access prompts, outputs, models, and adapters?
- Where are logs retained?
- How is evaluation evidence stored?
